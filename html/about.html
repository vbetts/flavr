About

Flavr makes it quick and painless to access your own recipe database from anywhere, at any time

Whether you're planning your grocery shopping for the week, or deciding what you want on the spur of the moment, your own custom cookbook is at your fingertips.

You can store and search your recipes by ingredient keyword, category or title. If you're preparing a meal for friends with special dietary needs, you aren't sure what you want for dinner that night, or you just want to use up whatever ingredients are in your fridge, Flavr can help.
